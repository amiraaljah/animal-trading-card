Horse

The primary sensory input in horses is sight. The importance of vision is reflected in the size of the equine eye, which is the largest of any land mammal.That is why they are capable of seeing nearly 360 degrees at one time.

  • Scientic Name: Equus caballus
  • Average Lifespan: 25 to 30 years
  • Diet: herbivores
  • Weight: 54 to 998 kg

Horses exhibit a diverse array of coat colors and distinctive markings.Horses are mammals with hooves. They also have long tails, short hair, muscular torsos, long thick necks and elongated heads. Due to domestication, they are found all over the world.
